Job Finder App


Project Overview

A user-freindly, smart and quick job searching mobile application that will provide the best service in Ethiopia. It will help people to get their desired job. People can’t find job circular in their field and can apply easily. Applicants can get a reminder for their interview and get it online.

"Find out what you like doing best and get someone to pay you for it." - Katharine Whitehorn
Role & Respnsibility
  • UI|UX Designer
  • Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, determining information architecture, and responsive design.​

The Problem Statement

Job seekers often face challenges like fake job postings, limited options, time-consuming application processes, low-paying offers, and unclear information about qualifications. Employers lack an easy and convenient platform for finding skilled candidates or managing recruitment-related activities.

Product Goal

Design an easy-to-use job finding app that connects job seekers with their ideal jobs based on skills and preferences, while enabling employers to quickly find and hire suitable candidates.

The main goal of this platform is to connect job seekers with employers and make the job search and application process more efficient and convenient for both parties.


Research

Competitive Analysis

The initial step in my research was to understand successful patterns in current products and missing gaps in unique values. I explored 10 products and narrowed down to 5 top competitors. I analyzed the good and bad of each competitor, and performed our points of analysis on: on-boarding experience, homepage, features, filter system.


User Interviews

I interviewed 8 users in an average of 20 minutes to ask some questions about their experience when looking for a job, I found a lot of interesting data. Therefore, my target users would be employers and young professionals, especially those who don’t have jobs.


Persona

Based on the research insights, I developed a primary persona demonstrating my target users, especially their goals and pain points or frustration.


Ideation

I did a quick ideation exercise to come up with ideas for how to address user needs.

Lo-Fi Wireframe

After ideating and drafting some paper wireframes, I created the initial designs for the app. These designs focused on personalized  guidance to users to help manage their jobs.

Hi-Fi Wireframe

Moving forward with the process, I turned my Lo-Fi wireframes into Hi-Fi Wireframe.


HI-Fidelity Prototypes

In this phase, I iterated my design based on low-fidelity validation findings. I discovered that there was still some confusion around how to search for job types, bookmark jobs, or save jobs in the app. As a result, I focused on improving these aspects while finalizing our UI design.
